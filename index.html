Chest Pain Order Set Prioritization

Front-End Care Standardization Task Force · Multidimensional Delphi on 24 candidate initiatives (13 order-set design elements and 11 data-analysis work packages).
Method: Two-round modified Delphi with Effort × Impact × Interest rating per initiative  ·  Panel: Full task force (~15 members)  ·  Output: Panel-scored Value Matrix for EBP Committee submission

How this survey works

  1. Review the data. Open the Data Dashboard tab to see how we currently order on chest pain patients in our ED.
  2. Rate 24 proposed ideas — data-informed suggestions for new analytics or informatics builds — on three axes:
    Effort (1-5)
    How much work would this take? 1 = trivial, 5 = multi-month build.
    Impact (1-5)
    How much would this improve patient care, quality measures, or ED operations? 1 = marginal, 5 = transformative.
    Interest (1-5)
    How personally invested are you to work on this? 1 = not me, 5 = I'd lead it.
  3. Submit before our next meeting. Generate your code on the R1 Rating tab and email it to Tehreem.

What happens next

  1. Next meeting. The Quality Workgroup will walk through the evidence supporting or opposing individual components of the chest pain order set.
  2. Round 2 survey. After the meeting, you'll re-rate the same 24 items with fresh context.
  3. Output. Final Value Matrix → EBP Committee → System Medical Directors.
